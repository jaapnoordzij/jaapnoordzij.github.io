Silence - Shusaku Endo

It seems that in 2016 Martin Scorcese's film Silence will finally be screened. Good reason to read the book, since it has been on my "To Read List" for a long time.

Being a Christian myself the book is quite unsettling but for other reasons than I thought. In 1644 two Jesuit priests from Portugal, father Francisco Garrpe and father Sebastian Rodrigues, travel from Macao to Japan.
