The Alchemist - Paulo Coelho

Now and then you must read Paulo Coelho's books. Not that they are great literature, they are definitely not, but only because they make you feel good. Coelho has a very simple recipe: Take some juicy stories from the Bible, add a good measure of magical-mythical mumble jumble and attach a hero (heroine) that takes itself far too serious. And the best thing is that he present his stories as a kind of moral lessons on how to have a better life.
